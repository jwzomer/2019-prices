Palmer & Sicard Inc., Serving Northern New England Since 1954

Why choose Palmer and Sicard?

At Palmer and Sicard, we exceed client expectations every day. The company is 100-percent employee-owned, therefore every employee is an owner with a vested interest in customer satisfaction. Our team of employee owners work together in a highly collaborative environment to be one of the best mechanical contracting firms in New England. At Palmer and Sicard we help you keep your business moving forward.

Exceptional attention to detail, quality workmanship, dependable customer service, and pride of ownership are core values of our business. We guide our clients from concept through successful finished product by tailoring each project schedule, budget, and deliverable to meet our clients specific needs.

Building mechanical systems is our specialty but we believe that the most important thing we can build is relationships with our customers. Through the years, hundreds of schools, hospitals, office complexes, manufacturing plants, hotels, municipalities, and active living retirement communities have put their trust in Palmer and Sicard. Whether handling design/build or plan & specification projects, our talented team of experts has the skills, technology and experience to meet any challenge.

We would love to hear about your project goals, contact us today.
